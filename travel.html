Travel Tips for Kerala

Helpful advice to plan your trip smart, safe, and smooth.

Quick Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Carry light cotton clothing; it's humid in most regions
  • Use bottled water and pack mosquito repellent
  • Houseboat bookings are best done in advance
  • Respect local customs, especially in temples
  • Public transport is affordable but check timings
