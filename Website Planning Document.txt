# Website Planning Document

Title: Kerala Travel Blog

Purpose: To showcase the culture, food, and beautiful destinations of Kerala through a personal travel blog.

Audience: Travelers, students, or anyone curious about Kerala.

Content Goals:
- Highlight key destinations with visuals and links to things to do in those places
- Share traditional culture and food with descriptions and images
- Include a contact form for feedback and queries (using Formspree)

Tools:
- HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap 5
- Images hosted locally
- GitHub Pages for deployment
