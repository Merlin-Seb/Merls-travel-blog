Top Places to Visit in Kerala

Munnar

Lush green tea gardens, misty hills, and peaceful valleys.

Alleppey

Experience houseboat cruises through tranquil backwaters.

Wayanad

Explore caves, waterfalls, and forest trails in the Western Ghats.

Athirapally

Witness Kerala’s most iconic waterfall surrounded by lush forest.

Kochi

A vibrant port city blending colonial architecture and modern art.

Kovalam

Relax on golden sands and enjoy sunsets on Kerala’s coastline.
